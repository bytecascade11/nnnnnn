# Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra: Early 2026 Rumors Are Already Here

The S25 Ultra launches in two weeks, but the rumor mill never sleeps.  
Here's what trusted leakers are saying about Samsung's 2026 flagship.

### Battery & Charging
- 5,000 mAh (no capacity bump from S25 Ultra)
- 60W wired charging (up from 45W)
- 25W wireless (up from 15W) + full Qi2 support

### Camera Upgrades
- New 12MP 3× telephoto (up from 10MP)
- Possible variable aperture return
- 200MP main + 50MP ultrawide carry over with better processing

### Design
- Even rounder corners than S25 Ultra
- Slimmer body (~8.2 mm), lighter titanium frame
- Vertical camera island with raised bump
- New colors: Black, gray, silver-blue, deep purple, orange

### Performance
- Snapdragon 8 Gen 4 (global exclusive)
- RAM: 12GB base, up to 16GB
- One UI 8.5 with deeper Galaxy AI 3.0

### Price & Launch
- Expected: $1,299 starting
- Reveal: Late January 2026
- Release: Mid-February 2026
