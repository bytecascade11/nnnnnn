# Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra: All the Latest Leaks (December 2025)

Samsung’s next flagship is just weeks away (rumored January 22, 2025 launch) and the leaks are coming in hot.

### Design & Build
- Titanium frame with **rounded corners** for better grip (goodbye sharp edges!)
- New color options: Titanium Black, Gray, Green, Silver
- Slightly thinner bezels → 6.8" display feels bigger

### Camera
- 200MP main sensor (same as S24 Ultra)
- 50MP ultra-wide (huge upgrade from 12MP)
- 10MP 3× and 50MP 5× periscope lenses
- Rumored 3000nits brightness **debunked** — still 2600nits peak

### Performance
- Snapdragon 8 Elite (3nm) — up to 45% faster GPU
- 12GB RAM standard
- One UI 7 + Android 15 out of the box
- 7 years of OS + security updates

### Battery & Charging
- 5,000mAh battery
- 45W wired, 25W wireless
- First Samsung phone with **Qi2** (MagSafe-style) charging

### Price & Launch
- Expected starting price: $1,299
- Pre-orders right after January Unpacked
- Shipping: late January / early February 2025

Samsung is going all-in on AI this year — expect Galaxy AI 2.0 with real-time translation, advanced photo editing, and more.
